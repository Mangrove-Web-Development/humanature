Real growth comes from the inside

Creating a truly human-centered culture that embraces empathy, creativity, and well-being can be a daunting task. But the consequences of not addressing these issues can lead to low employee engagement, reduced team cohesion, and a less innovative work environment.

Our inneraction lab is designed to take a deep dive into how your organization functions, to help drive growth in human capital and develop inclusive, thriving work environments.

Package details

Sign up for one of three offerings—a 2-, 6- or 12-session package. Each tier includes experiential learning sessions, 1:1 coaching opportunities, peer-to-peer group sessions, and individualized organizational design strategy consulting.

How we can help

Our Inneraction Lab can help your organization:

  • Develop an understanding of people-centered organizational cultures that promote inclusion and belonging.

  • Identify and understand the 4 dimensions of human development.

  • Recognize and address the effects of trauma and chronic stress in the workplace.

  • Implement debiasing techniques to promote inclusive decision-making.

  • Identify and apply strategies for fostering an inclusive and equitable work environment.

  • Equip participants with the ability to identify situations that call for the application of debiasing techniques and support their implementation.

  • Evaluate the effectiveness of DEIB initiatives and identify areas for improvement.

When you're ready

If you're ready to invest in the growth and well-being of your organization and its employees, Inneraction Lab is here to help.

What We Believe

We have to get this right

Our mission is to put humanity at the heart of every organization— right where it belongs. We imagine a world where leaders and their employees don’t merely make it through each week, but thrive. That’s why we’ve developed and shaped a wide range of processes and tools that empower us to do just that.

Using solutions drawn from sources as wide-reaching as the Indigenous world and cognitive neuroscience, we support teams in designing cultures, implementing learning opportunities, and building models of practice that foster an environment of learning and compassionate curiosity. 

At Humanature, we believe that equity is a process as much as it is an outcome. We believe all of this knowledge is paramount to how we, as a world, think about human development, belongingness, and the future of society. Most of all, we believe we’ll get it right. We have to.
