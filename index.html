Design for Humanity

We are a design strategy firm that works with leaders and their organizations to put humans at the center.

What We're About

Our company works with organizations, their employees, and other stakeholders to transform how they understand and support human development, interaction, and performance.

We develop and share know-how and tools that enable clients to address their mointractable challenges where inadequate soft/behavioral skills meet issues of stress, social cognitive bias, and race-equity-and inclusion (REI). Organizations can do this because we have seen it and we have helped to make it happen.

Humanature Methodology

At Humanature, our work is grounded in a methodology that we call “Design for Humanity”.

Our approach is based upon and informed by current social science research. We have established a tight yet flexible framework of inquiry, practice and education. Further, the tools that we deploy sit at the intersection of human, economic, and ecological resilience.

We draw on insights from the human sciences to help our clients create the conditions for the flourishing of core human attributes and behavioral skills. These are the attributes and skills that support creativity, resilience, empathy, and meaningful engagement.
